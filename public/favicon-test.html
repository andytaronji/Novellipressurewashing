Troubleshooting

If you don't see the favicon in your browser tab:

  1. Try hard refreshing your browser (Ctrl+F5 or Cmd+Shift+R)
  2. Clear your browser cache
  3. Check if the images above are loading correctly
  4. Inspect the page source to ensure the favicon references are correct
